About SRK Institute

SRK Institute of Management and Computer Application, Sapeda, is a reputed institution offering professional courses in the field of Management and Computer Applications. Affiliated with Saurashtra University, the college is known for its dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and emphasis on holistic development. The institute nurtures students by organizing cultural events, technical fests, sports meets, and social drives. It aims to provide quality education combined with co-curricular activities to ensure all-round development of its students.
